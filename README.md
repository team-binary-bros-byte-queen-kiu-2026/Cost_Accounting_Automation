## Project Overview

Cost accounting in many post-Soviet systems still relies on outdated, manual processes that have remained largely unchanged for decades. Professionals often work with hundreds or even thousands of static tables—frequently in PDF format—and depend heavily on personal experience to locate and interpret the necessary data. This approach is time-consuming, inefficient, and difficult to scale.

Our project aims to modernize this process by transforming fragmented cost accounting tables into a structured, centralized data source. By consolidating and organizing this information into a unified system, we enable faster access, improved accuracy, and better usability.

On top of this foundation, we introduce an AI-powered assistant designed to support cost accountants in their daily work. The system will allow users to query data, retrieve relevant information instantly, and receive guidance on cost calculations and decisions. Instead of manually searching through documents, accountants can interact with the system in a more intuitive and efficient way.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between legacy workflows and modern technology, making cost accounting more accessible, scalable, and intelligent.
